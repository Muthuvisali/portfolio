Problem Statement

The Challenge in
Supply Chain Finance

Supply chain finance teams make thousands of daily invoicing and payment decisions that directly impact working capital, all while maintaining strict policy compliance. Manual review is slow, error-prone, and doesn't scale. Simple rule engines lack the reasoning capability needed for nuanced supplier scenarios.

Speed vs Compliance

Autonomy vs Control

The Opportunity — Inspired by Honeywell × JPMorgan

Honeywell partnered with JPMorgan to deploy a record-breaking $900M+ supply chain finance solution across 260+ suppliers in 7 currencies, freeing up significant working capital through optimized payment terms. This project builds on that real-world problem: a multi-agent AI system where each agent reasons independently, then a governance agent validates ALL decisions against policy before approving them — bringing AI reasoning to the scale and compliance demands of enterprise treasury operations.

1
Invoice Analysis
Validates invoice integrity
  • Validates invoice against purchase order
  • Checks supplier enrollment and contract terms
  • Detects anomalies: price variance >10%, qty mismatches, unusual payment terms
Output: APPROVED or FLAGGED status
2
Supplier Risk
Composite risk scoring 0–100
  • Analyzes payment history (% early/on-time/late, avg days late)
  • Checks delivery performance (on-time rate, defect rate)
  • Assesses financial health (credit score, cash conversion cycle)
  • Calculates composite risk score from 0 (no risk) to 100 (extreme)
Output: Risk score + LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH/CRITICAL
3
Working Capital Optimizer
Cash flow modeling
  • Calculates cash impact of different payment term scenarios
  • Models supplier cash flow impact if terms change
  • Validates against treasury thresholds (max term extension, policy limits)
  • Recommends optimal payment terms to balance cash freed with supplier relationship
Output: WC freed ($) + recommended term adjustment
4
Financing Recommender
Payment method optimization
  • Gets current market rates for supply chain financing (SCF discounts, fees)
  • Evaluates NORMAL (standard terms), SCF, and IMMEDIATE (early payment) methods
  • Calculates financial trade-offs for each method
  • Recommends optimal method based on supplier tier and risk profile
Output: Recommended payment method + discount/fee applied
5
Executive Summary
CFO-ready insights
  • Aggregates batch performance across all invoices processed in period
  • Calculates KPIs: % approved auto, % escalated, WC freed, SCF penetration
  • Identifies trends and outliers across the supplier portfolio
  • Generates CFO-ready insights and recommendations for next period
Output: Dashboard metrics + strategic recommendations
6
Governance Agent
The critical validation layer
  • Check 1: Confidence validation, are all 5 prior agents >80% confident?
  • Check 2: Policy compliance, does risk level match payment method?
  • Check 3: Contradiction detection between agent outputs
  • Check 4: Required fields all present and valid?
  • Check 5: Escalation triggers, amount >$5M or risk >75?
  • Check 6: Audit trail, generate compliance-ready trace of all checks
Output: APPROVED / REQUIRES_REVIEW / REJECTED / ESCALATE_TO_CFO

The Differentiator

How the Governance
Layer Works

The Governance Agent is what separates this from a typical multi-agent system. Most systems stop after the last agent outputs a recommendation. This system adds a validation step that catches everything that could go wrong before any decision is approved or executed.

1
Confidence Check
Are all upstream agents >80% confident in their outputs? Low confidence flags for human review before proceeding.
2
Policy Check
Does the risk score align with the recommended payment method? A high-risk supplier with normal terms gets rejected.
3
Contradiction Check
Are there logical conflicts between agent recommendations? Contradictions are flagged rather than silently passed through.
4
Completeness Check
Are all required fields present and valid? Missing data in the decision chain triggers a required review.
5
Escalation Check
Does this decision trigger CFO review? Amount >$5M or risk score >75 auto-escalates regardless of other checks.
6
Audit Check
Generate a compliance-ready trace of the entire decision with all reasoning and checks documented.
IF amount > $5M→ ESCALATE_TO_CFO
IF payment_method = "immediate"→ ESCALATE_TO_CFO
IF risk > 75 AND method = "normal"→ REJECTED
IF any agent confidence < 80%→ REQUIRES_REVIEW
IF any policy check failed→ REQUIRES_REVIEW
ELSE→ APPROVED
